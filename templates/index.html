What is a cryptic hunt?

Cryptic hunts are basically scavenger hunts but on a virtual platform with the internet at your disposal.

What are lead confirmations?

There's a certain period of time in a hunt when you are allowed to ask to moderators if you are on the right track or to validate whatever you have discovered in a level.

Is there any limitation to the number of tries?

No, you can try anything you feel could be the answer to the question. However, moderators can disable your account incase they get to know that the platform is being brutforced as everything is being logged.

What is the format for answers?

The general format is all lowercase, no blankspaces and no special characters. However, if you forget to do so, the platform will interpret it in the correct format itself.

I am new to cryptic hunts. What is it all about?

Refer to the CodeTech Cryptic Hunt resource to get a basic knowhow of how cryptic hunts function. One always learns with practice and experience.
